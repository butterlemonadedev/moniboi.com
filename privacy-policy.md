# Privacy Policy

**App:** Moniboi
**Developer:** Butterlemonade
**Contact:** info@butterlemonade.com
**Effective Date:** 2026-03-06

---

## Summary

Moniboi itself does not collect personal information. However, our advertising partner Google AdMob may collect certain data as described below. Your save game data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

---

## Information We Collect

Moniboi itself does not collect personal information. However, our advertising partner Google AdMob may collect certain data as described below.

Moniboi does not collect, store, or transmit:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Usage analytics or telemetry (from the app itself)
- Crash reports or diagnostic data (from the app itself)

---

## Local Storage

The only data stored by Moniboi is your save game data (high scores and game state). This data is stored locally on your device using Unity's `Application.persistentDataPath`. It never leaves your device and is not accessible to Butterlemonade or any third party.

---

## Network Access

Moniboi connects to Google AdMob servers to display advertisements. These connections transmit standard network data (IP address, device identifiers) to Google's ad-serving infrastructure. No other network calls are made by the app.

---

## Advertising

Moniboi displays banner advertisements and optional rewarded video advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data including:

- Device advertising identifier (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
- IP address
- Device make, model, and operating system version
- General location (derived from IP, not GPS)
- App usage data related to ad interactions

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Moniboi does not have access to the data collected by AdMob.

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

- **Android:** Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS:** Settings > Privacy > Tracking > disable tracking for Moniboi

---

## Analytics and Tracking

Moniboi does not include its own analytics, tracking pixels, or behavioral monitoring. Google AdMob may track ad interaction data as described in the Advertising section above.

---

## Third-Party SDKs

Moniboi integrates the following third-party SDKs:

- **Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob):** Used to display advertisements. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on data collection practices.
- **Unity Engine:** Operates entirely on-device with no data collection.

---

## Children's Privacy

Moniboi is rated Teen (ESRB T / PEGI 12) due to themes of dark humor, poverty, and crime. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

---

## Data Security

Your save game data remains on your device under the standard protections provided by your operating system. Advertising data transmitted to Google AdMob is encrypted in transit via HTTPS.
